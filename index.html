GU Exchange

GU Exchange is a locally run marketplace app for trading Gods Unchained cards on the IMX marketplace.

The current version offers the following features:

  • Buying, selling and transferring Gods Unchained cards and currencies on IMX
  • Completing decklists based on missing cards on your GU account.
  • Selling or transferring entire GU collections.
  • Trading with specific players by looking up their connected wallet address.
